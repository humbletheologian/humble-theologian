Serious theology without the ego.

Humble Theologian is a free resource for Bible college students and thoughtful Christians who want to learn Scripture, love people well and live the faith with humility.

It is about learning, loving and living the faith: thinking carefully, loving God and neighbour and letting theology become faithful discipleship in ordinary life.

The site is designed as a solo theology library: organised enough to be useful, personal enough to remain honest and humble.

Plain-language guides to the gospel, essential Christian beliefs, salvation, resurrection, new creation and the hope Christians confess.

Bible and Interpretation

Reading Scripture carefully

Resources on how we got the Bible, how to interpret it responsibly, how translations differ and how to move from ancient context to faithful application today.

Christian Living and Formation

Learning, loving and living

Reflections on discipleship, humility, prayer, ordinary faithfulness and the kind of Christian maturity that joins truth, love and action.
