# Privacy

Humble Theologian exists to provide free articles, Bible studies and devotional resources. The site is not designed to collect personal information from readers.

## Email contact

If you email me through the contact details on this site, I will use your email address and message only to read and respond to what you have sent. I will not add you to a mailing list without your permission.

## Analytics

The site may use simple, privacy-conscious analytics to understand which resources are being used and how readers are finding the site. The purpose is to improve the usefulness of the site, not to identify individual readers.

## External links

This site links to external websites, books, organisations and resources. Those sites have their own privacy practices, and I do not control what they collect or how they use it.

## No sale of personal data

Humble Theologian does not sell personal information.

## Future changes

If the site later adds features such as financial support, newsletter subscriptions or more detailed analytics, this page should be updated so readers know what is being collected and why.
