# Humble Theologian Category Guide

Use these broad categories for article front matter:

- Core Christian Beliefs
- Bible and Interpretation
- Disability, Faith and Church
- Hell, Judgment and Hope
- Church and Pastoral Care
- Christian Living and Formation
- Personal Reflections

Use `topic:` for the more specific subject, such as `Old Testament Law`, `Hermeneutics and application`, `Creeds, confessions and doctrine`, or `Biblical theology and eschatology`.

Avoid creating new categories unless you also update `topics.md`.
