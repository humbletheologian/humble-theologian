# Humble Theologian Category Guide

Use these broad categories for article front matter:

- Core Christian Beliefs
- Bible and Interpretation
- Disability, Faith and Church
- Hell, Judgment and Hope
- Church and Pastoral Care
- Christian Living and Formation
- Personal Reflections

Use `topic:` for the more specific subject, such as `Old Testament Law`, `Hermeneutics and application`, `Creeds, confessions and doctrine`, or `Biblical theology and eschatology`.

Avoid creating new categories unless you also update `topics.md`.


## Article Types

Use `article_type:` to tell readers what kind of article they are opening.

- Foundational Guide - explanatory articles introducing a doctrine, biblical topic or interpretive question.
- Where I Stand - articles explaining Shayne's current position while acknowledging other Christian views.
- Personal Reflection - reflective pieces shaped by experience, culture or an ongoing question.
- Ministry Guide - practical resources for church life, pastoral care, discipleship or ministry practice.
- Book Recommendation - reviews and recommendations centred on books or resources.

Article type does not replace `category:` or `topic:`.
