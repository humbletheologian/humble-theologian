# Devotions

These devotional resources are for readers who want Scripture, prayer and reflection to shape ordinary life. Some are original Humble Theologian resources. Others are public-domain classics made easier to find and use.

The aim is not to replace careful Bible study or church life, but to support daily faithfulness.

## How to use these resources

You might use these devotions:

- for personal prayer and reflection
- as a short reading before Bible study
- in small groups or pastoral care settings
- as newsletter reflections
- as a way back into Scripture during a dry season

Devotions are not meant to be a substitute for Scripture. They are meant to lead us back to Scripture, prayer and faithful discipleship.
