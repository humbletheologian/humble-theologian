**Love as a Verb** is the devotional heart of Humble Theologian.

Christian love is not merely something we admire, feel or discuss. Love is something we practise. It becomes visible in patience, service, forgiveness, prayer, hospitality, pastoral care and ordinary faithfulness.

The original Humble Theologian site included a full dated devotional archive. For the relaunch, these are published as normal web pages rather than bulky PDFs so they are easier to read on mobile, easier to search and easier to link to directly.

## Why this series matters

Theology should never become a substitute for love. The more deeply we learn Scripture, the more faithfully we should love God and neighbour. This series exists to keep those things together: learning, loving and living the faith.
