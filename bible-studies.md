# Bible Studies

These studies are written for thoughtful Christians, group leaders and readers who want to go deeper than a surface-level discussion, while still remaining accessible to people who are newer to Christian faith.

They are designed to stretch without discouraging. Each study includes context, observation, interpretation, application, prayer and optional further reading.

## How to use these studies

Each session works best if people read the passage before the group meets. The questions are not designed merely to find the right answer. They are designed to help people listen carefully to Scripture, examine assumptions and respond faithfully.

Group leaders do not need to answer every question. Choose the ones that best serve the group. Leave room for prayer, silence and honest conversation.
